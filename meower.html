Welcome to Meower Cat Hotel!


We're thrilled to offer your feline friend a purrfect stay at our luxurious cat hotel. Our facilities are designed to provide a cozy and comfortable experience for your cat, whether they're staying with us for just a night or for an extended stay.


At Meower, we know that cats are unique and special creatures, which is why we offer a variety of accommodations to suit their individual needs. From private rooms with plush bedding to spacious communal areas for socializing, our hotel has everything your cat needs to feel at home.

About Meower

Meower was founded by a team of passionate cat lovers who wanted to create a safe and welcoming space for cats while their owners are away. Our team has years of experience in cat care, and we're dedicated to providing the best possible experience for your feline friend.


Our experienced and compassionate staff are dedicated to providing top-notch care for your furry friend. We offer daily playtime and grooming, as well as regular health check-ups to ensure your cat stays happy and healthy throughout their stay.


We understand how important it is to stay connected with your cat while you're away, which is why we offer regular updates and photos so you can see how your furry friend is doing.
